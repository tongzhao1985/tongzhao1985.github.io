BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

I received his B.Sc., Ph.D. degrees in computer science and technology, from Beijing Institute of Technology, Hunan University, China, in 2007,and 2014, respectively. Currently, I am an associate professor with the College of Information Science and Technology, Hunan Normal University, Changsha, Hunan, China. My research interests include High Performance Computing, Internet-of-Things, edge computing, and combinatorial optimization. I am a member of the IEEE and a senior member of the CCF.

I am looking for self-motivated Master students who are interested in my research areas. Please feel free to contact me, if you want to join my research group.
