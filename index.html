BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

Zhao Tong (Member, IEEE) was born in Yueyang, Hunan, China. He received a B.S. degree in computer science from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2007, and a Ph.D. degree in computer science from the Hunan University in 2014. He is currently an associate professor in the College of Information and Engineering at Hunan Normal University. He was a visiting scholar with the Georgia State University from 2017 to 2018. He award the Distinguished Youth Scholar Science Foundation of Hunan Province, China, and the Second Award of Natural science of Hunan Province, China. His research is supported by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, Natural Science Foundation of Hunan Province. His research interests include parallel and distributed computing systems, resource management, Big Data, and machine learning algorithm. He has published more than 35 research papers in the international conferences and journals, such as IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Internet of Things Journal, Information Sciences, FGCS, JPDC, PDCAT, etc. He is also a senior member of the ChinaComputer Federation.

I am looking for self-motivated Master students who are interested in my research areas. Please feel free to contact me, if you want to join my research group.
